Our Process

1. CONTACT 2. DISCOVERY 3. DESIGN 4. CODING 5. LAUNCH

Let's hear it

We kick off things with a cup of coffee, a plain sheet of paper, and an eager product manager who is ready to listen to your big idea. Our team of product managers is made up of experienced product developers with a technical background, they listen to your idea, and take you through our process and what we can bring to the table.

Setting the foundation

With 5 years of experience in this industry, we know very well that a lot needs to happen before writing the first line of code.
This is why we help our clients to mitigate risk by placing focus on their business and customers over technology through what we call project discovery.The discovery process enables us to review your business, plan and present relevant research and grasp your brand before we identify the most suitable solutions to problems.

Ultimately, this helps us to answer strategic questions concerning your product vision, target market and more. Our team works with you to understand your business, customers, and challenges before delivering our findings and suggestions.

Designing Wireframes

Forget what they told you, Beauty does matter, and to us, it is a whole religion. Our design team is always in creative mode. Forget what they told you, Beauty does matter, and to us, it is a whole religion. Our design team is always in creative mode.

Getting Dirty

This is the most interesting part, now that we know exactly the solution we are going to build, our team spreads the work within weekly sprints and gets to work. Our product manager keeps you in the loop every week to keep you updated on the progress and let you know in case the team needs some input from you.

Testing, feedback, and iteration is a culture at Awesomity, projects are closely monitored for quality and tested from start to finish while maintaining a proper timeline.

Final Product

We help you with everything from launch to upkeep, assisting in app store optimization, hosting, maintenance and more, making product launch a stress-free experience. It’s not our style to build a digital product and then walk away. To be truly successful, it needs to fit your business objectives and smoothly integrate with what you’ve already got.

Not just that, but our strategic partnerships see us work with customers over the long-term to help our partners scale and develop their product, as well as identifying how tech can solve other problems your company faces.
